ATO rounding order

Rounding rules live in rules/rounding.yaml. GST is rounded at the line level to the nearest cent (HALF_UP) before totals are rounded to whole dollars for BAS labels. PAYGW uses the per-period table rounding (weekly stays at cents, monthly rounds to whole dollars) before labels are rounded to whole dollars.
