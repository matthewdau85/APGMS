PCI Security Standards Council Bulletin: Updated Version of Mobile Payments on COTS (MPoC) to Address Errata 17 February 2023 The PCI SSC has published MPoC v1.0.1, an updated version of the Mobile Payments on COTS Standard, which addresses minor revisions including errata. The new version includes the following changes: • Addresses various typographical errors • Adds missing ‘tick’ symbols from the Domain 5 section of the applicability matrix • Adds missing Domain 4 items to an A&M Service Provider part of applicability matrix • Adds missing ‘not’ from requirement 1D-4.3 MPoC v1.0.1 is now available in the PCI SSC Document Library.
