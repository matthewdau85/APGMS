The final meeting for this focus group was held on 30 August 2023. This group is now closed. This working group will identify, discuss and co-design solutions to enable Single Touch Payroll (STP) employer totals to be adjusted. The working group will seek to understand changes required to better support reporters of STP when: submitting the adjustment event using their software, or an adjustment event is submitted, to validate any necessary changes required to an activity statement before or after it has been lodged. The STP Adjustment service working group’s outcomes will be a collective recommendation to the ATO’s Single Touch Payroll team.
