Activity Statement 2023 service remediation working group – 28 August 2024 Original Published Date 19/09/2024 - 9:21am Tax Category Payroll Tax preparation Business accounting Description The Activity Statement 2023 service remediation working group is an ATO initiative that will engage with industry representatives to explore pathways to remediate the activity statement 2023 service to ensure we have a fit for purpose service for DSPs. This meeting was held online on Wednesday 28 August 2024 . Key outcomes Summary Below is a summary of key outcomes. Note: The full key outcomes and presentation materials are available in the DSP Hub . The group was welcomed to the first meeting of the Activity Statement 2023 (AS2023) service remediation working group. DSPs were thanked for their insights and feedback so far. Overview of options for consultation Overview given of the options presented at the Digital Service Provider Architecture Reference Group (DARG) held 31 July 2024. It was agreed to progress Option 1 - Remediate Activity Statement 2023, including the MST and XML Schema. AS2009 and the remediated AS2023 services will be available simultaneously to allow for issue resolution. Next steps The next session will be a workshop in Canberra on 5 September 2024. For any questions or feedback please contact the secretariat by email at DPO@ato.gov.au or lodge a ticket in DSP service desk . All versions No previous versions available.
