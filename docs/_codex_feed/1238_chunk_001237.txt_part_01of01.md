Activity Statement 2023 service remediation working group – 31 October 2024 Original Published Date 14/10/2024 - 12:41pm Tax Category Payroll Tax preparation Business accounting Description The Activity Statement 2023 service remediation working group is an ATO initiative that will engage with industry representatives to explore pathways to remediate the activity statement 2023 service to ensure we have a fit for purpose service for DSPs. This meeting was held online on Thursday 31 October 2024. Key outcomes Summary Below is a summary of the key outcomes. Note: The full outcomes are available on the DSP hub . Status of AS remediation work The ATO has commenced work on the AS2025 service. Delivery is on track for March 2025 EVTE release and June 2025 production release. High level comparison of behaviour The group was provided with a technical overview of the changes in behaviour of the new AS2025 service and a comparison of the AS2009 and AS2023 services. Key discussions covered: Overview of the new vs old services and their purpose/role Simplified Submit response payload layout and guiding principles Proposed names for the VR and MST artefacts Updates to the AS2025 Get response schema with new attributes Impact of xsi:nilattribute introduction in AS2025 service, including a sample payload of the AS2025 Get response Open discussion Discussions covered the following: AS2009, ASLRPT2014, ITCRPT2014 and LDGLST2016 deprecation date of 31 December 2026 Draft AS2025 technical artefacts published on the DSP Hub for review. Next steps The group will reconvene in 2025 with the next working group meeting to be held on 16 January 2025 . For any questions or feedback, contact the secretariat by email at DPO@ato.gov.au or lodge a ticket in DSP service desk . All versions No previous versions available. Last modified date 13 Dec 2024
