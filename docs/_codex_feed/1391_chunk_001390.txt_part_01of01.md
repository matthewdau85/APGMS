The SuperStream standard enables superannuation payments and data to be sent from employers to superannuation funds and between super funds and the ATO in a standard electronic format. SuperStream makes superannuation contributions and rollovers quicker, easier and more efficient. As of October 2017, all rollovers and contributions between government and super funds are now electronic and in accordance with the SuperStream standard.
