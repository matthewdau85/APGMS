How should PCI DSS v4.x requirements noted as superseded by another requirement be reported after 31 March 2025? After 31 March 2025, superseded requirements should be marked as Not Applicable (N/A) in a Report on Compliance (ROC) or Self-Assessment Questionnaire (SAQ). Three PCI DSS v4.x requirements include a note that the requirement will be superseded by another requirement as of 31 March 2025.
