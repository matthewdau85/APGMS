Activity Statement 2023 service remediation working group The modernisation of the activity statements service was implemented in June 2023 as part of the BAS processing known amounts (PKA) initiative. The intent of the project was to improve the quality of data returned and redesign the service for an accurate and tailored activity statement reporting experience. This working group will engage with industry representatives to explore pathways to remediate the activity statement 2023 service to ensure we have a fit for purpose service for DSPs. Membership Membership of the Activity Statement 2023 service remediation working group, is made up of digital service providers who provide business management and tax preparation software. Consultation The ATO consultation framework underpins our consultation framework arrangements. 2025 2024 Contact information DSPs can provide feedback or ask questions by raising a ticket via the DSP service desk . The secretariat of the working group can be contacted by email at DPO@ato.gov.au . Last modified date 17 Dec 2024
