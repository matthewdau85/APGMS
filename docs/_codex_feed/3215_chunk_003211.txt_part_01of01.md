PCI Security Standards Council Bulletin: PCI DSS v3.2.1 Minor Updates Published to Include All PCI Participating Payment Brands 7 October 2022 Minor updates have been made to PCI DSS v3.2.1 and its supporting documents to reflect the terminology and logos of all PCI Participating Payment Brands. The updated documents include the PCI DSS v3.2.1 Report on Compliance (ROC) Template, Attestations of Compliance (AOCs) and Self-Assessment Questionnaires (SAQ), and Prioritized Approach. Any questions about the impact on an entity’s assessment related to these updates should be directed to the entity’s acquirer or the payment brands. A revision number has been added to each document to reflect this update. Please refer to the Document Changes Table at the start of the document for detail.
