Vendors and Service Providers, and How They Function Small businesses/merchants may come into contact with a number of payment vendors or services providers, and it is important for merchants to understand the type of vendor they are working with and ensure the vendor has taken appropriate steps to protect card data. The table on page 2 describes the most common types of payment vendors and service providers and what merchants should look for with each vendor.
